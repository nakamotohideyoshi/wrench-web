Campaign Information

  • A campaign is a promotional activity that promotes a product or service. To begin setting one up for success, we recommend you create two custom matches to kickoff your campaign process.
  • Create a product match: this match type identifies leads on your list who are likely to highly correlate with you product, so you have a prioritized list of people to target. In the description provide some or all of the following:
    • Product description, including the value proposition and benefits
    • Competitor products
  • Create a people match: this match type allows us to get to know your leads, to begin forming a "sticky" messaging strategy. In the description provide some or all of the following for approximately 20 customers:
    • Current customer job titles
    • Current customer industries
    • Current customer job functions
    • Current customer LinkedIn in profile links
